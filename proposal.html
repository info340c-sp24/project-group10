Emotunes

David Xu, Rohan Simha, Arav Manchanda

App Description

The users of the application are college music fans/listeners, who intend to find new music based on an emotion-based algorithm. As stated above, music can play a strong role in influencing emotion, so thus those looking for music based on this can utilize our application. This application is inclusive and seeks to improve the mental health of all users to utilize it, therefore minority groups could also benefit from our app to reduce stress and relax.

One of our features will allow the user to input multiple songs, and then it will analyze the emotion of such songs, and then input more song recommendations of a similar emotion theme. Another feature will be the option for the user to input a specific emotion from a list, and then recommend songs based on that inputted emotion. The last feature will be an option to order the outputted songs based on different factors, such as alphabetical order, or by other factors. The users will be able to access song information based on their input and preferences.

Our application will be using Spotify API to provide more specific song recommendations and a better user experience. Using the different Spotify metrics such as song “danceability”, tempo, and “key”, the app will be able to analyze song emotion and recommend songs based on this.

This application could assist college music fans in reaping the benefits of how different emotions of music can accommodate one's emotions and mental health. By providing users with songs with similar moods to those they have input, as well as recommending songs that align with their preferences, our application could contribute to enhancing emotional status and mental health.
